IMPORTANT WORKFLOW CORRECTION

Do not skip directly from the Landing Screen into processing or organization states.

The workflow must correctly transition into the Upload Portal immediately after the user clicks:

“Start Organizing”

The current prototype is incorrectly bypassing the document-upload step.

The upload workflow is a required and meaningful part of the emotional and operational product experience.

The correct workflow structure should be:

1. Landing Screen
2. Upload Portal
3. Upload State / Upload Confirmation
4. Processing Screen
5. Intake Summary Screen

Do not automatically imply records are being organized before the user uploads documents.

The user must first:

* open the Upload Portal
* select or drag documents
* see upload confirmation/progress
* then intentionally begin organization

The emotional progression should feel like:

“I need help organizing this.”
↓
“Here are my records.”
↓
“It’s organizing everything.”
↓
“My records finally make sense.”
