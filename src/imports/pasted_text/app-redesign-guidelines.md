1. UPLOAD BOX ACTIVE STATE

After files are dragged or selected, the upload box should immediately react.

Use:

soft pulse animation
soft glow sweep animation
subtle scale movement
low-opacity muted slate shimmer
smooth easing

The upload box should transform from an empty dashed state into an active confirmation state.

Inside the upload box, show:
✔ Files uploaded successfully

Also show small internal category feedback such as:
✔ Pay Records detected
✔ Workplace Communications detected
✔ HR Documents detected

Do not make users scroll before they understand files were received.
