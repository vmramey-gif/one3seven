# one3Seven — Mobile App Design Prompt

Create a sleek, mobile-first app called **one3Seven**.

one3Seven is a modern document organization and intake preparation platform designed to help California remote workers organize employment-related records into structured timelines and intake summaries.

The app should feel:

* calm
* premium
* organized
* supportive
* trustworthy
* modern
* clear
* human

The overall experience should communicate:
“Your records are finally becoming easier to understand.”

The app should NOT feel:

* overly legal
* aggressive
* intimidating
* robotic
* overly cheerful
* flashy “AI”
* futuristic or sci-fi

Avoid:

* gavels
* courtrooms
* scales of justice
* lawsuit imagery
* neon AI aesthetics
* anything implying legal conclusions or legal outcomes

Use:

* soft grayscale palette
* muted slate or deep blue accents
* rounded cards
* subtle shadows
* smooth motion
* connected workflow visuals
* clean spacing
* elegant typography
* structured timeline layouts
* soft operational animations

The emotional feeling should be:
messy records → calm structure → organized clarity

one3Seven does not provide legal advice, determine legal rights, predict legal outcomes, or establish attorney-client relationships.

The platform is designed to assist with:

* document organization
* intake preparation
* timeline building
* workflow structuring
* record categorization

The tone throughout the app should feel:

* calm-professional
* lightly conversational
* reassuring
* concise
* natural
* composed

Think:
quietly helpful assistant

Not:
chatty startup app
