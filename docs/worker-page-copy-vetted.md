## THE PROBLEM (worker's point of view)

Right now, getting help means digging through your phone and email, trying to remember dates, and explaining the same thing over and over — often while waiting days for a callback. Important details get lost, and it's hard to know where to even start.

one3seven takes that weight off you: you put your records in one place, and it organizes them so you arrive ready instead of overwhelmed.

---

## HOW IT WORKS

**1 · Start whenever you're ready**
No appointment, no phone tag. You go at your own pace and can stop and come back anytime.

**2 · Tell your story and add your records**
Answer a few plain-language questions — what happened, when, who was involved, and what's written down. Upload whatever you have: pay stubs, emails, texts, schedules, warning letters, a termination notice. A photo of paper works too. Don't have something? Just add what you have.

**3 · See your timeline, organized**
Your records and story are arranged into a clear, dated timeline, with each fact linked to the document it came from — so your story is easy to follow.

**4 · You choose who receives it**
Nothing is shared until you decide. Send your organized intake to a firm you choose, or submit it through a firm's own intake link. You're in control of who sees it.

---

## YOU'RE IN CONTROL

**Your records stay private**
Your intake is not shared with any firm until you approve sharing or submit through that firm's intake link.

**Plain language, always**
No legal jargon. Your status updates are written in plain English, and you can check them anytime.

**You decide who receives it**
You choose which firm to send your organized intake to — directly, or through a firm's intake link.

**No chatbot**
You fill out a guided form at your own pace — you're not talking to an AI pretending to be a lawyer or a firm. AI helps organize your records after you submit.

---

## WHAT IT IS (and isn't)

one3seven helps you **organize** your employment records into a clear packet. That's it — and that's a lot.

- It does **not** give legal advice.
- It does **not** decide your legal situation or whether you have a case.
- It does **not** predict outcomes or amounts.
- It does **not** replace talking to a licensed attorney.

An attorney reviews your organized records and decides what they mean. one3seven just makes sure you arrive organized instead of scattered.
