# one3seven — Copy Style Guide (UPL / fee-splitting / claims safety)

**Read this BEFORE drafting any customer-facing copy** — pages, scripts, cold emails, the Ask-AI
prompt, demo text, packet labels. This is a pre-draft checklist, not a post-ship review step. Three
copy rounds in this build needed a legal pullback; the fix is catching it at draft time.

Customer-facing = anything a worker, a firm, or a prospect could read or a rep could say on a call.

---

## The one rule: the verb test

For every customer-facing sentence, look at the **verb** attributed to one3seven. If the verb ascribes
a **legal act, conclusion, or judgment** to the product, STOP and rewrite.

- ❌ **Unsafe verbs** (one3seven must never do these): conclude · advise · recommend · rank · select ·
  route · match · steer · score · value · predict · determine · win · maximize · represent · guarantee ·
  assess claim strength · decide the matter.
- ✅ **Safe verbs** (what one3seven actually does): organize · reflect · surface · categorize · link ·
  build a timeline · structure · preserve · extract (facts from records) · flag (for attorney review).

The tension to name every time: sales/persuasion instinct reaches for stronger, more confident language;
that instinct is in constant conflict with the verb test. When in doubt, weaker verb wins.

---

## Avoid → Use

| ❌ Avoid | Why | ✅ Use instead |
|---|---|---|
| "you never open an AI" / "you never see the technology" / "invisible AI" (as a claim it isn't AI) | Misleading — it IS an AI product | "no chatbot required" · "you don't have to prompt a chatbot" · "you open the organized file" |
| "you represent" | Presumes the attorney-client representation | "you verify, you decide" · "attorney judgment stays with your team" |
| "we route / match / rank / steer / select / connect workers to attorneys" | Reads as a lawyer referral service (regulated) | "the worker shares their own intake link" · "not a lawyer referral service; does not recommend, rank, or select attorneys for workers" |
| "maximize case value" · "get paid faster" · "win" · "recover more" · any $ or outcome promise | Outcome/revenue guarantee → UPL + false-advertising risk | "decide faster" · "less unbillable triage time" · "no waiting in limbo" (velocity = workflow, never outcomes) |
| "you have a case" · case scoring · claim valuation · settlement recommendation · "strong/weak evidence" | Legal conclusion | "information that may help" · "items requiring confirmation" · "organized for attorney review" |
| "damages" | Legal conclusion / valuation | "wage-exposure estimate — arithmetic from records, attorney work-product" |
| "unverified" (labeling provenance) | Names facts by worth | name by SOURCE: "Source-linked" · "Document on file" · "Worker-stated" |
| "confidence" (on extractions) | Implies a judgment score | drop it; state the source instead |
| "California-approved AI" · "the AI platform California adopted statewide" · any gov-endorsement vibe | Implies government endorsement | "Built on Anthropic's Claude" (factual, lower on page, never in hero) |
| "partner / certified / endorsed by Anthropic" · Anthropic logos | Trademark / false-affiliation | "Built on / powered by Anthropic's Claude" only |
| Fabricated metrics ("15 minutes", "6 schemas", "100% traceable") · fake scarcity counters | False advertising | only defensible, real numbers ("0 legal conclusions", "source documents preserved") |
| "independently verified" · "audited" · "penetration tested" · "SOC 2" (as a security claim) | Unverifiable without an actual audit/pen test on file | state the plain mechanism only: "Row-level database policies enforce separation between firms." Add a verification claim ONLY once a real audit exists to point to |
| A public cohort size ("50 firms", "first 100") pitched as a marketing number | It's really a **support-capacity promise** — one3seven is sole-engineer-supported; an unsupportable number is a false commitment | pick a number you can actually onboard hands-on in the window, or say none ("a small founding cohort … a few at a time"); make the horizon explicit |

## The recurring risk lanes (what keeps getting caught)

1. **UPL** — one3seven organizes; it never advises, concludes, scores, values, or predicts. The attorney
   does all legal judgment. Say "organizes and reflects — it never concludes."
2. **Fee-splitting / referral** — one3seven is a **software tool on a flat subscription**, never per-client,
   never a referral service. It does not recommend, rank, select, route, or match workers to attorneys.
   The worker controls their own records and their own intake link.
3. **Endorsement over-claim** — factual "Built on Anthropic's Claude" is fine; anything implying California
   or Anthropic *endorses* one3seven is not. Keep credibility lines factual and off the hero.
4. **Overclaiming** — no outcome/money promises, no fabricated metrics, no fake scarcity.

## "Partner" is safe — with a boundary
"Intake partner, shaped around your firm" is fine: it means a **business** partner that adapts to the firm
(configuration + onboarding + attention). It must NEVER imply one3seven practices law, is a *legal* partner,
or shares in the firm's fees or case outcomes. "Shaped around your firm" = tuning/config, never bespoke
promises of custom engineering.

---

## Standing instruction for future drafting
Before writing any customer-facing page, script, prompt, email, or label: run it through the verb test above
and scan against the Avoid list. Flag borderline cases to the founder rather than shipping — and when the
founder finally books the attorney session, have the attorney bless (or correct) this doc so it becomes the
authoritative source.
