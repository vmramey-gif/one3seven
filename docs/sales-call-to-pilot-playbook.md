# Sales Playbook — Call → Pilot (one page)

**The line you never cross:** one3seven *organizes and reflects — it never concludes.* No "you have a case," no valuations, no outcome predictions. The attorney evaluates everything. (This is also why it's safe.)

**Work order:** A-priority firms first → Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Tier 3 → Tier 4.
**Every card now has an email.** Lead with the sample packet: **one3seven.com/demo**

---

## The 7 stages (CRM stage in caps)

**0 · TARGET — the list**
Open the card → read the tier **Call Brief** (angle + objection prep are pre-written).
→ *Advance: make first contact.*

**1 · CONTACTED — first touch**
Call or email with the Call Brief angle. Lead with the **/demo link** — it sells harder than any pitch. Fast-log the outcome → this **claims the firm** (drops off the shared list).
→ *Advance: firm replies or picks up. Goal: get a human response.*

**2 · CONVO — real conversation**
Sell the value, not features: "4–6 hours of triage arrives structured, before your first call." Handle objections from the brief. **One ask: book a 15-min screen-share.** Not pricing — the appointment.
→ *Advance: a demo time is set.*

**3 · DEMO_BOOKED — appointment set**
Set the follow-up date. Send a calendar hold + the /demo link as a teaser.
→ *Advance: the demo happens.*

**4 · DEMO_DONE — they've seen it**
Walk the live /demo packet: chronology → source links → clarifications → "never concludes." **Define the finish line together:** "By day 7 you'll have reviewed 3 organized intakes and tell me if it saved time." Offer the **free 7-day pilot, no card.**
→ *Advance: they say yes.*

**5 · PILOT — 7 days live**
Log stage → `pilot`, follow-up = day 7. (No commission yet.)
Founder/engineer provisions the firm (firm_code + trialing plan) and tests one intake end-to-end.
Firm shares its intake link with 3–5 incoming clients. Watch `pilot_submits`; light day-3 check-in.
→ *Advance: the day-7 debrief.*

**6 · PAID — convert**
Debrief: walk one real organized intake → ask the success question → present pricing (Solo $199 / Practice $499 / Firm $899). **Commission (20% residual) starts here.**
→ `paid` or `nurture`.

## 🔥 Inbound lane (highest priority)
A firm that fills out **/for-firms** auto-lands as a **priority-A card at TARGET with today's follow-up**. They've raised their hand — **claim and call FAST** (inbound cools within hours). Skip stages 0–1.

Open card → read Call Brief → call/email with the /demo link → fast-log to claim → set next follow-up. Repeat. A-priority first.
