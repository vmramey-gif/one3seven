## HOW IT WORKS

**1 · Send the intake link**
One link per worker. Your firm controls who receives it. Workers complete intake on their own time — no scheduling friction, no phone tag.

**2 · Worker tells their story and uploads records**
Guided questions help capture dates, people, documents, and sequence. Documents upload directly. Audio, if used, is transcribed and then the audio file is discarded. ⚠ VERIFY audio behavior matches this exactly.

**3 · Your firm opens the organized packet**
A dated timeline with surfaced facts linked back to source materials. Key quotes. Document coverage. Clarifying questions. Built for your review — not the worker's legal conclusions.

**4 · You evaluate**
No AI legal advice. No case-strength score. No outcome prediction. Just the worker's facts — dated, organized, and sourced — so your firm applies its own judgment.

> You begin review faster: less time assembling records before the first conversation — without replacing the first call.

## WHY ONE3SEVEN — built differently from legal-drafting AI

Many legal AI tools try to draft, analyze, score, or recommend. That creates risk: hallucinated citations, unverified legal output, and blurred responsibility.

one3seven is intentionally narrower:
- We do **not** draft legal documents.
- We do **not** talk to clients as a chatbot.
- We do **not** score case merit.
- We do **not** estimate outcomes.
- We do **not** recommend attorneys.
- We do **not** tell workers what claims they have.

We organize the intake record so the attorney can evaluate it.

## AI + LEGAL SAFETY

**Designed around human accountability**
one3seven is built around a clear review chain: the worker provides the story and records, AI assists with organization, and the attorney evaluates. The system does not make autonomous legal decisions, draft filings, or replace professional judgment.

**Built for auditability**
Every surfaced fact links back to the worker's input or a source document, so review never depends on trusting a black-box summary.

**AI foundation**
Built on Anthropic's Claude — the same AI assistant California recently made broadly available to state agencies and local governments through a statewide partnership. ⚠ VERIFY wording with counsel before publishing; never imply Anthropic endorsement or California affiliation.

**Firm data**
Each firm sees only its own intakes; row-level database policies enforce separation between firms. Uploaded documents are used to organize the intake and are not used to train AI models. Transcripts remain only as part of the firm-controlled intake record.
