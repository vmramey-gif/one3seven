# Sales Authority — What You Can and Cannot Commit To

**From:** Victoria Ramey, one3seven / One3Seven Ventures LLC
**To:** Tadmor "Tad" Russell
**Date:** 2026-07-13
**Re:** Scope of authority in firm sales conversations (interim, pending our signed founder + independent-contractor agreements)

This memo defines exactly what you are and are not authorized to promise firms on one3seven's behalf.
It exists to protect both of us: a commitment you make verbally can bind the company, so we need the
binding commitments to live in the written agreements a firm signs — not in a sales call.

## The one rule
**You are not authorized to make binding commitments on behalf of One3Seven Ventures LLC.** Every term
that actually binds the company must come from a written agreement the firm signs (the Firm Subscription
Agreement / Founding-Firm Order Form). In conversation, you describe; the paperwork commits.

## Specifically, do NOT promise any of the following (this list is not exhaustive, but these are the ones that have come up):

**Pricing**
- No "locked for life," "forever," "grandfathered forever," or any indefinite price guarantee.
- No specific founder/discount price unless it is stated in the current approved order form. Founder
  pricing terms live ONLY in the signed Founding-Firm Order Form.
- No custom discounts, waived fees, refunds, or price exceptions without Victoria's prior written approval.

**Timelines & delivery**
- No delivery or turnaround guarantees ("organized within X minutes/hours," "ready by [date]").
- No promises that a specific feature will be built or shipped by a specific date.

**Exclusivity**
- No "you'll be the only firm in your city/region/practice area," or any territorial or category exclusivity.

**Outcomes (also a verb-test / UPL issue)**
- No results claims of any kind: not "win more," "recover more," "settle faster," "fewer denials," "get
  paid faster," or anything implying one3seven improves case outcomes or revenue.
- Speed language is about workflow only ("decide faster," "less unbillable triage time") — never outcomes.

**Legal / data**
- No legal advice, coverage opinions, or characterization of any matter's strength.
- No data-security or privacy promises beyond exactly what the published Privacy Policy says.
- Never state or imply that Anthropic, California, or any government endorsed, certified, partnered with,
  or approved one3seven. "Built on Anthropic's Claude" is the only allowed phrasing.

## What to do instead
- Quote only from currently approved materials (the /for-firms page, the vetted copy, the sample packet).
- For any special request, exception, or question you're unsure about, say: **"Let me confirm that and get
  back to you"** and bring it to Victoria before committing.
- Make clear to firms that all terms are set out in the agreement they'll sign, and that the signed
  agreement — not our conversation — governs.

## Interim status
This memo is interim guidance. It will be superseded by our formal Independent Contractor Agreement and
Founder Agreement, which will define your role, authority, commission, and equity. Until those are signed,
this memo governs what you may represent to firms.
