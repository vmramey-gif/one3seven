What this means

If a firm screens 100 inquiries per month and accepts fewer than 5, they spend intake hours on 95 cases that will never convert. At 2–4 hours of triage per inquiry, that is 190–380 staff hours per month on work that generates zero revenue.

one3Seven reduces the triage time on rejected cases from hours to minutes — recovering that capacity without changing the firm's acceptance criteria.
