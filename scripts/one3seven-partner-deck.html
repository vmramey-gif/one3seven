02 · The Platform
⚡
What one3Seven actually does
A two-sided platform built specifically for employment law intake

The core idea

one3Seven is the infrastructure layer between workers who need legal help and employment law firms who need to find the right cases efficiently. We don't give legal advice. We don't tell firms which cases to take. We make sure the right information reaches the right attorney in the clearest possible format — and that both sides know what happens next.

📋
Guided Worker Intake
Workers complete a structured intake flow at their own pace — employment timeline, key people, what happened, document uploads. No phone call required. No intake form lost in email. Everything captured in one place.
🧠
AI Intelligence Layer
The platform reads uploaded documents, extracts key quotes, identifies retaliation timing gaps (e.g., "termination 34 days after HR complaint"), surfaces confirmed facts, and flags what's still missing. Every intake arrives pre-analyzed.
⚖️
Firm Review Dashboard
Attorneys see a clean, structured intake card: overview, timeline, AI intelligence, categorized documents, readiness signals, and gaps. Review takes 10–20 minutes — not 3–6 hours. One-click decision: accept, request more documents, or pass.
🔔
Closed-Loop Outcomes
Workers aren't left wondering. Firms have a documented record of every decision. The loop closes cleanly — with notifications, status updates, and document request workflows all handled in-platform.

Workers upload their actual files. The platform extracts quotes, identifies retaliation timing, and surfaces what's missing. The attorney sees not just what the worker said — but what the evidence shows. That's the difference between a summary and an intelligence layer.
